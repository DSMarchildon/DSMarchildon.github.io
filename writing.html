Giant's Wake Setting Overview

This is an overview of a setting I created for a Worldbuilding class.

I was assigned with a course-wide project that encompassed the entire semester; create a world from nothing. The class was based around modules where we would learn about various topics on a weekly basis. Topics like mapmaking, general story narrative, history building, and even languages. Giant's Wake is the final product that I produced at the end of the semester.


The Last Throne

This is a short story I created that is a part of my Giant's Wake setting that I created for my Worldbuilding class.

The short story details the life of a Dwarven stonesmith that is tasked with carving out a dead King's last throne.
